Amur Leopard

Conservation Status: Critically Endangered

Native to the temperate forests of Eastern Russia.

- Solitary predator
- Known for its beautiful coat
- Population in the wild: ~100

Vaquita

Conservation Status: Critically Endangered

The world's smallest and most endangered marine mammal.

- Fewer than 10 individuals remain
- Found only in the northern part of the Gulf of California
- Lives in shallow coastal waters

Learn more about the Vaquita

Threatened by bycatch in fishing nets
