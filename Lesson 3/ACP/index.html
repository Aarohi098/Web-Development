Greek Mythological Creatures

Minotaur
A half-man, half-bull creature trapped in the Labyrinth. It was slain by the hero Theseus.
Cyclops
One-eyed giants, known for their strength. The most famous, Polyphemus, was blinded by Odysseus.
Cerberus
A three-headed dog guarding the gates of the Underworld. Hercules subdued him as part of his Twelve Labors.
Hydra
A multi-headed serpent that regrows two heads for every one cut off. It was defeated by Hercules.
Chimera
A fire-breathing creature with a lion's body, a goat's head, and a snake's tail. Killed by Bellerophon.
Pegasus
A winged horse born from Medusa's blood. It was tamed by the hero Bellerophon.
Sirens
Bird-women who lured sailors to their doom with enchanting songs. Encountered by Odysseus.
Medusa
A Gorgon with snakes for hair whose gaze turns people to stone. She was killed by Perseus.
