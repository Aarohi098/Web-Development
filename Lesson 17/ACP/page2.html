Decorative Plants

Monstera (Monstera deliciosa)

Large, split leaves give this plant a tropical, bold look — a favorite in modern home décor.

Fiddle Leaf Fig (Ficus lyrata)

Known for its big, violin-shaped leaves and striking height — great as a dramatic indoor centerpiece.

Calathea (Calathea orbifolia)

Features round, patterned leaves with light and dark green stripes — eye-catching and elegant.

ZZ Plant (Zamioculcas zamiifolia)

Glossy, dark green leaves on upright stems — low-maintenance and sleek for any stylish space.

Pilea (Pilea peperomioides)

Also called the “Chinese Money Plant,” its round coin-like leaves make it playful and trendy.

Anthurium (Anthurium andraeanum)

Bright red or pink “flowers” (actually spathes) and glossy leaves — adds a pop of color indoors.
