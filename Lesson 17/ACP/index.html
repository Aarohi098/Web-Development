Air Purifying Plants

Snake Plant (Sansevieria)

A hardy plant known for its upright, sword-like leaves. Great for beginners and purifies indoor air.

Spider Plant (Chlorophytum comosum)

A fast-growing plant with arching leaves and baby “spiders” that hang down. Easy to care for.

Peace Lily (Spathiphyllum)

Famous for its glossy leaves and white flowers. Thrives indoors and removes toxins effectively.

Aloe Vera

Succulent with thick, fleshy leaves that store water. Known for soothing skin and improving air quality.

Boston Fern (Nephrolepis exaltata)

A lush, feathery fern that loves humidity. Great for adding greenery and freshening air in shady spots.

Rubber Plant (Ficus elastica)

A tall plant with large, shiny leaves. Durable and excellent at removing indoor pollutants.
