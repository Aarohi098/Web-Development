A green guide for every space — big or small.

Edible Plants

Basil (Ocimum basilicum)

Basil is a herb used in a wide variety of dishes. It grows well in sunny garden spots and adds an aromatic touch to meals.

Tomato (Solanum lycopersicum)

Juicy and versatile fruit used in salads, sauces, and sandwiches. Needs sunlight and support.

Mint (Mentha spicata)

A refreshing herb used in drinks and cooking. Fast-growing and great for containers.

Lettuce (Lactuca sativa)

A leafy green ideal for salads and sandwiches. Grows quickly and loves cool weather.

Chilli Pepper (Capsicum annuum)

Adds heat and flavor to meals. Comes in many shapes and spice levels — loves the sun.

Strawberry (Fragaria × ananassa)

Sweet, red berries loved by many. Grows well in pots or garden beds with plenty of sun.
